How much are you lifting?*


 

 

The Barbell Weight Calculator is a quick and easy way to figure out what combination of weight you require for today's lift.

*The calculator assumes you are using a 45lb bar and you have 45, 25, 10, 5, and 2.5 pound weights only available for use.
