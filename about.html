My full name is Alexander Manuel Vallejo, and I was born on December 3rd, 1992. I have lived my entire life around the Pittsburgh area. I attended Our Lady of the Sacred Heart High School where I was the director of the school news studio, a member of NHS, and a varsity volleyball player. Also while in high school I was an active member in Boy Scout Troop 335 and achieved the rank of Eagle Scout at age fifteen. I began attending the Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh in the fall of 2011.

In college I major in computer engineering and maintain a GPA in the top 5% of my department while participating in several extra-ciricular activities. I have served as: Secretary of my fraternity (Delta Chi), Freshmen Engineering Peer advisor, Technical Director of the Green Hope Orphanage Inc, and president of the Pitt chapter of IEEE.

In the summer of 2013 I worked as a software engineer intern for KIT Solutions where I developed an internal timesheet application. In the summer of 2014 I will be working for Microsoft as a SDET for the Windows Services division. I could not be more excited to move to Seattle for the summer and work for Microsoft! It has been a dream of mine to work for Microsoft (or another top tech company) since 2008. Lots of hard work, determination and especially persistance got me to where I am today.
