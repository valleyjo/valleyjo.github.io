My full name is Alexander Manuel Vallejo, and I was born on December 3rd, 1992. I have lived my entire life around the Pittsburgh area, and I am looking forward to moving away when I graduate. I like Pittsburgh, but I would also like a little change of pace.

I attended Our Lady of the Sacred Heart High School where I was the director of the school news studio, a member of NHS, and a varsity volleyball player. Also while in high school I was an active member in Boy Scout Troop 335 and achieved the rank of Eagle Scout at age fifteen. My interest in science and technology really took off while I was in high school, and I decided to pursue a career in computer engineering. I began attending the Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh in the fall of 2011.

Many long hours of hard work and dedication helped me realize the degree of commitment required to become a professional engineer. My efforts and especially my determination landed my name on the Dean's List! After much consideration, I am dedicated to my future profession. I am looking forward to my future with excitment.
