Weekly Information:

  • Week 1

Random advice:

  • When you email professors include the class you're in as well as the time it meets.
  • Make sure you put your name in your email client so people know who is sending them emails! People don't know who amg63@pitt.edu is!
  • GPA counts! Find electives that interest you so they are naturally easier for you.
  • Establish a time management strategy early. Experiment with different techniques and find what works for you.
  • Keep track of your grades and know what classes are "most important" in terms of impact on GPA.
  • Use failure as an oppertunity to improve yourself.
